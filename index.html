Kohlowala Ki Tareekh (History)

Kohlowala, Pakistan ke sooba Punjab ke zila Gujranwala ka ek mashhoor aur pur-aman gaon hai (Union Council No. 72). Yeh ilaka na sirf apni zarkhaiz zameen aur sunehri faslon ke liye jana jata hai, balki iski sabse bari pehchan yahan ka kapron ka karobar aur dasti karhai (embroidery) hai. Yahan ki mehnati khwateen apne hathon se kapron par aisi khoobsurat aur nafees karhai karti hain jiski dhoom poore Gujranwala belt mein hai.
